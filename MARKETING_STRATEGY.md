## 🎯 Week 1-2: Launch & Build Initial Traction

### 1. ProductHunt Launch (Day 1-3)
**Why**: 50,000+ makers see ProductHunt daily. Perfect for SaaS launches.

**How**:
1. Go to https://producthunt.com
2. Click "Ship" → Create your product page
3. Product name: "ContentAI - AI Marketing Copy Generator"
4. Tagline: "Generate high-converting marketing copy in 5 seconds"
5. Thumbnail: Create a clean image (use Canva.com, takes 5 mins)
6. Description: Focus on the problem you solve
   ```
   "Generating marketing copy takes hours. ContentAI does it in seconds.
   - Email campaigns
   - Social media posts
   - Google/Facebook ads
   - Landing page copy
   
   Perfect for freelancers, agencies, and small business owners."
   ```
7. Add your app URL + Twitter handle
8. **Launch at 12:01 AM PT on Tuesday** (best timing)

**Promotion**:
- Ask your 20 closest friends to upvote on launch day
- Post on Twitter: "Just launched on ProductHunt! Trying to build something useful for marketers. Any feedback welcome? [link]"
- Reply to every comment (this gets you upvotes)

**Expected results**: 100-500 signups, 3-10 paying customers

## 💰 Month 1-3: Sales & Growth

### Pricing Strategy
```
Free Plan (Convert to Pro later):
- 5 generations/month
- 2 content types
- Basic support

Pro ($29/month):
- 100 generations/month
- All content types
- Email support
- 60% of users should convert here

Business ($79/month):
- Unlimited generations
- Priority support
- Advanced features
- 30% of users
- 
Enterprise ($299+/month):
- API access
- Team seats
- Dedicated support
- 10% of users
```

**Why these prices?**
- Most SaaS tools underprice 2-3x
- People expect to pay for value
- You're saving marketers 5+ hours/week
- That's worth $100+ in value per week

### Sales Tactics

**1. Offer 30% off first month**
- Removes friction for first-time buyers
- Gets them in the door
- They realize value, stay for months
- Do this for first 100 paying customers

**2. Free trial for email list**
- "7 days free, just enter your email"
- Build email list (most valuable asset)
- Follow up: "Here's what you can do..."

**3. Case studies**
- After someone gets a win, ask for a testimonial with screenshot
- Turn into a case study: "How [Name] generated 50 email variations in 5 minutes"
- Share on Twitter, blog, etc.

**4. Referral bonus**
- Give $50 credit for each paid referral
- Tell existing customers: "Know someone who writes marketing? Give them a referral link"
- Costs you $50, gets you a $29/month customer = ROI in 2 months
